My name is Winkie.

I'm a graphic designer and digital illustrator living in Vancouver, BC. I am currently majoring in Interactive Arts and Technology with a concentration in Design and minoring in Publishing at Simon Fraser University. Before I entered SFU, I thought design was just drawing. However, the education from SFU has led me to accumulate more experiences in different categories of design, especially user experience design (UX). It also helps me to explore which type of designer I would like to be and taught me the ability to use different software, such as Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Indesign, SolidWorks, etc. My aspiration is to become a graphic and product designer who could design a product by combining a pragmatic approach with aesthetics, while meeting the user's actual needs and bringing warmth and happiness to users.
