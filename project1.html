01 Spring 2020

The Clown On A Unicycle

Modelling + Graphics


Project Description

This project is done in group. We created an Animated Beautiful Model (ABM) called The Clown On A Unicycle (Figure 1) by using SolidWorks. The concept of this model inspires the character, Billy from the horror movie Jigsaw and the character and Krusty the Clown from The Simpsons.

Figure 1: Ng, W.(Spring 2020). Final Project of The Clown On A Unicycle. [Screenshot]

Figure 2: Ng, W.(Spring 2020). Sketches of The Clown On A Unicycle. [Digital Scan]

Purpose

We decided to make a clown model riding on an unicycle because we wanted to create a funny, playful, lively and joyful model. We also hoped this model could cheer people up when they see this colourful model, especially the kids.


Design Process

Sketches

We brainstormed about how to connect the mechanism to the body parts of the clown by sketching out each parts of the model and discussed how big or how long should the parts be in order to prevent collisions between parts. We also decided what kinds of animation would be performed from this model, which is wheel rotation, legs motion and right hand motion.

SolidWorks

Based on the sketches, we created the parts separately and then connected them in SolidWorks (Figure 3).

Chellenge

Measuring the distance between different parts is the hardest in this project, such as connecting the four-bar linkage to the hands. It took time to measure and tested the most suitable length of the four-bar linkage and the position of the rods in order to make the hands move.

Reflection

Throughout this project, I learnt how to use sketches to communicate. During the design process, my teammate and I had come up with different ideas, so we put our ideas on paper and discussed which one we should choose based on the mechanism, material for the model, and which one we like. I think choosing the one we both like is important, otherwise, we will lose our passion to design the model.
