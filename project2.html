Minion Surgery

Digital + Graphics

In IAT 265, I created Minion Surgery (Figure 1) for my final individual project. The concept of this simulation is to help the Minion to do a heart transplantation for replacing the old heart. There will be some information listed at the top of the screen and users need to follow the steps to complete this simulation. The goal of this simulation is to help Minion by dragging and clicking the screen. This assignment taught me how to design and develop a game that integrates game design theories, computing concepts, and media techniques learnt from this course.
