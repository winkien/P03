02 Summer 2020

Minion Surgery

Digital + Graphics


Project Description

I created a simulation called Minion Surgery (Figure 1) by using Java. The concept of this simulation is to help the Minion to do a heart transplantation for replacing the old heart. There will be some information listed at the top of the screen and users need to follow the steps to complete the simulation. The goal of this simulation is to help Minion to complete its surgery by dragging and clicking the screen.

Figure 1: Ng, W.(Summer 2020). Introduction of Minion Surgery. [Screenshot]

Figure 2: Ng, W.(Summer 2020). Proposal of Minion Surgery. [PDF]

Design Process

I decided to make a surgery simulation. I created a proposal to describe the concept and goal of this simulation. Based on the description, I brainstormed the major coding, process and how could the users interact by using the mouse to click and drag.


Challenge

In order to meet the technical requirements, such as Perlin noise, recursive function, animation and decorator pattern, the simulation took a bit of time to run. The technical requirements also slow down the game process. Since the objects in the simulation are a bit small, it is hard for users to drag the objects to the proper locations. In order to solve these problems, I reduced the complexity of the technical elements and changed some of the dragging actions into clicking actions.

Reflection

This assignment allow me to design and develop a simulation that focus on interactive human activities and dynamic environmental variations by combining my own imagination and creativities and the coding technics. It also taught me how to manage my time by breaking this process into different tasks since this project was very time-consuming.
